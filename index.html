About Us

Welcome to Serendib Spice & Grill, where culinary excellence meets elegant dining. For over 15 years, we have been serving our community with the finest dishes crafted from the freshest ingredients.


Our passionate chefs combine traditional techniques with modern innovation to create unforgettable dining experiences. Whether you're joining us for a romantic dinner, family celebration, or business meeting, we promise exceptional service and extraordinary flavors.


At Serendib, every meal is a celebration, and every guest is family.
